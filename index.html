Who was him?

Steven Paul Jobs was an american businessman, inventor and investor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple ; The chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar ; a member of walt disney company's board of directors following its acquisition of Pixar; and the founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT.

And how he started Apple?

Steve jobs was a charismatic pioneer of the personal computer era. With Steve Wozniak, Jobs founded Apple in 1976 and transformed the company into a world leader in telecommunication. Widely considered a visionary and a genius, he oversaw the launch of such products as the iPod and the iPhone
